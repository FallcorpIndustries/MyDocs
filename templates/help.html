Help

Welcome to the Document Reader and Image Describer!

This application allows you to upload documents and images, generate summaries, and convert text to speech. Below is a guide on how to use the features available.

How to Use

  1. Upload Document:

    Use the "Choose File" button under "Upload Document" to select a .txt, .pdf, or .docx file. After selecting, press "Upload Document" to process the file.

  2. Upload Image:

    Use the "Choose File" button under "Upload Image" to select an image file. Supported formats include .png, .jpg, .jpeg, .bmp, and .gif. After selecting, press "Upload Image" to process the image.

  3. History:

    All your uploaded files, along with generated summaries and audio files, will appear in the History table. Navigate through your history to access these items.

  4. Summarize Document:

    In the History table, locate the document you wish to summarize and press the "Summarize" button next to it. The summary will be generated and added to your history.

  5. Generate Audio:

    To convert text to speech, press the "Generate Audio" button next to a document or its summary in the History table. The audio file will be created and listed alongside the text.

  6. Play Summary:

    If a summary has an associated audio file, press "Play Summary" to listen to it. Ensure your speakers are on and at an appropriate volume.

  7. Delete:

    To remove an item from your history, press the "Delete" button corresponding to that item. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Voice Commands

For a hands-free experience, you can use voice commands to navigate and interact with your documents. Here are the available commands:

  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition): Extract text from a specific page within a document.
    • English Examples: "Perform OCR on page 3.", "Extract text from page five."
    • French Examples: "Effectuer l'OCR sur la page 3.", "Extraire le texte de la page cinq."
  • Navigate to Heading: Jump to a specific heading in the document.
    • English Examples: "Go to heading 2.", "Jump to heading five."
    • French Examples: "Aller à l'entête 2.", "Sautez à l'entête cinq."
  • Summarize Document or Section: Generate a summary of the entire document or a specific section.
    • English Examples: "Summarize the document.", "Provide a summary of the introduction."
    • French Examples: "Résumer le document.", "Fournir un résumé de l'introduction."
  • Find Mentions or References: Locate mentions of specific keywords or topics.
    • English Examples: "Find mentions of artificial intelligence.", "Search for references to climate change."
    • French Examples: "Trouver les mentions de l'intelligence artificielle.", "Rechercher les références au changement climatique."
  • Compare Documents: Compare the current document with another to identify differences.
    • English Examples: "Compare with document 2.", "Find differences between document one and document three."
    • French Examples: "Comparer avec le document 2.", "Trouver les différences entre le document un et le document trois."
  • Read Bullet Points: Retrieve and read all bullet points or list items.
    • English Examples: "Read the bullet points.", "List all bullet items."
    • French Examples: "Lire les points de liste.", "Lister tous les éléments de la liste."
  • Skip References: Skip over reference sections to focus on the main content.
    • English Examples: "Skip all references.", "Ignore the references section."
    • French Examples: "Ignorer toutes les références.", "Ignorer la section des références."
  • Author Information: Retrieve information about the author of the document.
    • English Examples: "Who is the author?", "Tell me about the author."
    • French Examples: "Qui est l'auteur ?", "Parlez-moi de l'auteur."
  • Read the Title: Retrieve and read the title of the document.
    • English Examples: "Read the title.", "What is the name of the document?"
    • French Examples: "Lire le titre.", "Quel est le nom du document ?"

If a command requires additional information, the system will prompt you accordingly. For example:

  • Prompt for Missing Information:
    • English: "Please specify the page number for OCR."
    • French: "Veuillez préciser le numéro de page pour l'OCR."
  • Error Handling:
    • English: "Invalid page number."
    • French: "Numéro de page invalide."
  • Unrecognized Commands:
    • English: "Sorry, I didn't understand the command."
    • French: "Désolé, je n'ai pas compris la commande."
