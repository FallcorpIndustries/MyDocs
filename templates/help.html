Help

Welcome to the Document Reader and Image Describer!

How to Use:

  • Upload Document: Click on the "Choose File" button under "Upload Document" to select a .txt, .pdf, or .docx file. Then click "Upload Document" to process it.
  • Upload Image: Click on the "Choose File" button under "Upload Image" to select an image file. Supported formats are .png, .jpg, .jpeg, .bmp, and .gif. Then click "Upload Image" to process it.
  • History: All your uploaded files and generated summaries/audio will appear in the History table.
  • Summarize Document: Click the "Summarize" button next to a document in the History to generate a summary.
  • Generate Audio: Click the "Generate Audio" button next to a document or summary to convert text to speech.
  • Play Summary: If a summary has audio, click "Play Summary" to listen to it.
  • Delete: Click "Delete" to remove an item from the history.
